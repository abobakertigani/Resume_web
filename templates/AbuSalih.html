My Story

I graduated with a Bachelor's in Civil Engineering from University of Karary in 2021. Since then, I've been working on humanitarian projects focused on health and education across Darfur, blending engineering knowledge with Monitoring, Evaluation, Accountability, and Learning (MEAL) expertise.

I am continuously upgrading my skills through certifications in Google Data Analysis and Project Management, and I love training others in computer skills and data tools. My goal is to create sustainable, impactful solutions to improve lives in my community and beyond.
